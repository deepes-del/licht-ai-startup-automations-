Privacy Policy – Water Tracker

Last Updated: June 2026

Water Tracker respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected and used when you use the Water Tracker mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Name
  • Age
  • Weight
  • Profile Photo (Optional)
  • Daily Water Intake Records
  • Reminder Preferences

How We Use Information

  • Calculate daily water intake goals
  • Track hydration progress
  • Send reminder notifications
  • Personalize user experience

Data Storage

User data is stored locally on the user's device.

Water Tracker does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties.

Notifications

The application may send reminder notifications to help users maintain hydration goals.

Third-Party Services

The application may use Android system services for notifications and app functionality.

Children's Privacy

Water Tracker is not specifically directed toward children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected on this page.
